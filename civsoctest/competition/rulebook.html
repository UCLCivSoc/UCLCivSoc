RULEBOOK

Guidelines for the Termly Design Contest

Software Requirement

Designs should utilise AT LEAST 1 software used in the course (e.g., AutoCAD, Revit, ArcGIS, GSA, etc.). This encourages the application of skills learned during your studies.

Top-Down View

Pictures submitted must include a top-down view (plan view) of the building or site you are proposing. This helps in understanding the site context and layout.

Team Size

A maximum of 4 people are allowed in a team. Teams larger than 4 members will be disqualified.

Voting & Fairness

There is no voting limit per person, but we expect fair play. If there is sufficient proof of fraud or vote manipulation, the entry will be DISQUALIFIED.

Submission Deadline

All entries must be submitted by the deadline. Late submissions will not be accepted. Please check the countdown timer on the main page.

Originality

All designs must be your own original work. Plagiarism or submitting work that is not yours will result in immediate disqualification.

File Requirements

Images should be high resolution (e.g., JPG or PNG). Unclear or corrupted files may be rejected. You can upload up to 5 images.

Use of AI - Images

The use of AI for Image Generation is not allowed. If any image is clearly discerned as being AI-generated, the related entry shall be removed from contest.

Use of AI - Description

The use of AI for Descriptions is not allowed. We wish the designer⋅s of the project to truly explain themselves what they intend to do with their project.

Use of AI - Ideation

The use of AI for Ideation is allowed, with caution. Whilst it is expected for designer⋅s to design their own project, ideation can be assisted with AI, although clearly disclosed in the description of the project.

Intellectual Property

You maintain full ownership of your designs, even after the competition ends. You can at any moment retract yourself (or, in the case of a team submission, once super-majority agreement, or 67%, is reached) from the contest. Although the previous statements are true, you grant the Society the right to display your design on this website and our social media.
